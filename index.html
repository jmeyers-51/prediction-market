How This App Works
The Concept
AngelinaWatch is a prediction market for your friend group. You create events — things Angelina might or might not do — and players bet coins on the outcome. 1 coin = $1. At the end, winners collect from losers in cash using the Settlement screen on the Stats tab.
Roles
You (Admin) — create events, set odds, mark events live, resolve outcomes, manage bets.

Players — open the same link on their phone, pick YES/NO or OVER/UNDER, enter a wager, and place their bet. No account needed — they just type their name.
Creating & Managing Events
Bet Types
YES / NO Player bets whether something will happen or not.
OVER / UNDER Player bets whether a number will be higher or lower than a line you set. You also set a label (e.g. "3.5 drinks" or "10 minutes").
Event Status
Open Bets are being accepted.
Live The event is happening right now — still accepts bets, pulses red so everyone knows.
Closed No more bets — event hasn't been resolved yet.
Resolved Outcome has been set — bets are marked win or loss automatically.
Setting Odds
Odds represent the multiplier a player gets if they win. Higher odds = bigger payout = less likely outcome in your judgment.
YES odds: 2.00 means a $10 bet returns $20 (profit of $10)
NO odds: 1.50 means a $10 bet returns $15 (profit of $5)
Odds must be greater than 1.01. An odds of 1.00 would mean no profit at all. Typical values: 1.5 to 5.0 depending on how likely you think the outcome is.
How Players Place Bets
Step-by-step for players
1. Open the link on their phone.
2. On the Market tab, tap YES or NO (or OVER/UNDER) on any event — this adds it to their slip.
3. Tap Slip to review selections.
4. Choose Singles (each event bet separately) or Parlay (all legs combined into one bigger bet).
5. Enter a wager amount in coins — each coin = $1.
6. Tap Place Bet, enter their name, confirm.
7. Done — their bet is saved and visible on the Stats tab.
Singles vs Parlay
Singles — each event is a separate bet with its own wager. If one loses, the others are unaffected.

Parlay — all selected events are tied into one bet. The multipliers stack (multiply together). Higher potential payout but all legs must win or the whole bet is lost.
Parlay example:
Event A: YES at 2.00x
Event B: OVER at 1.80x
Combined multiplier: 2.00 x 1.80 = 3.60x
Wager: 50 coins — Payout if both win: 180 coins ($180)
Bet Calculations
Single Bet Payout
Payout = Wager x Odds
Profit = Payout - Wager

Example: 100 coins x 2.50 odds = 250 coins payout
Profit = 250 - 100 = 150 coins ($150)
Parlay Payout
Combined Multiplier = Odds1 x Odds2 x Odds3...
Payout = Wager x Combined Multiplier

Example: 2.00 x 1.70 x 3.00 = 10.20x combined
50 coins x 10.20 = 510 coins payout ($510)
All legs of a parlay must win. One loss = entire parlay is lost, regardless of the other outcomes.
Net Position (Leaderboard)
Net = Sum of all (Payout - Wager) for wins
- Sum of all Wagers for losses

Positive net = player is up money
Negative net = player owes money
Probability Bar on Each Event
The colored bar on each event shows where the actual money has gone, not the implied probability from odds. It is calculated from total coins wagered on YES vs NO.
YES% = YES volume / (YES volume + NO volume) x 100

Example: 300 coins on YES, 100 coins on NO
YES% = 300 / 400 = 75%
Resolving Events
How to Resolve
From the Market tab (while admin mode is on), tap Resolve on any open or live event. Pick the winning outcome. All pending bets on that event are automatically marked win or loss.

For parlays, the system checks if all legs are resolved. If all legs won, the parlay is marked win. If any leg lost, it is marked loss.
Manual Bet Override
In the All Bets section below, you can manually mark any pending bet as a win or loss. Use this if you need to override the automatic resolution for any reason.
Cash Settlement
How to Settle Up
Go to the Stats tab. The Cash Settlement section at the top shows exactly who owes money and who collects, in dollars (1 coin = $1).

Players with a positive net collect from the group. Players with a negative net owe the group. Use the Mark Paid button to track who has settled up in cash.
If any events are still pending, the settlement totals will show a warning. Resolve all events first for final numbers.
Admin Tools
Reset All Bets
Deletes all bets from the database and resets coin volumes on all events to zero. Events are kept. Use this to start a fresh round without losing your event list.
Factory Reset
Deletes everything — all events, all bets, all players. The app returns to a blank slate. This cannot be undone.
Passcode
The default passcode is admin. To change it, edit the line const PASS = 'admin' in the HTML file and re-upload to GitHub.
